Plot

The main character is Katie, a 17-year-old who has been kept indoors throughout the day due to a rare condition that renders even a tiny bit of sunlight fatal since infancy. When she meets Charlie, fate steps in and they start a summertime romance.

Reviews

"Midnight Sun is a tender romantic drama that explores the challenges of first love against the backdrop of a rare genetic condition. Bella Thorne and Patrick Schwarzenegger deliver heartfelt performances, though the plot occasionally leans towards predictable melodrama.

Scott Speer's Midnight Sun beautifully juxtaposes the tranquility of moonlit nights with the poignant struggles of its protagonists, Katie and Charlie. While it follows familiar romantic tropes, the film's sincere portrayal of their relationship makes it a touching watch.
